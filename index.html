AI-assisted legal judge for Zambia.

How it works

01

Submit the case

Both parties enter their names, factual submissions, and the relief they are seeking. The system screens the input for a valid legal dispute before proceeding.

02

Retrieve law and precedent

A retrieval agent searches a curated database of Zambian Acts of Parliament and past High Court judgments, building a cited legal dossier from verified local sources.

03

Analyse and reason

A judicial analysis agent applies the retrieved law to the case facts, conducting a procedural audit before ruling on the merits. All findings are anchored to retrieved precedent.

04

Receive the judgment

A formally structured judgment is produced following Zambian High Court conventions, complete with cited authorities, issue-by-issue analysis, and final orders alongside a validation report.
